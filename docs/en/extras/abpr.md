# ABetterRoutePlanner

"A Better Routeplanner" is a popular app (ABRP, accessible via web at [https://abetterrouteplanner.com](https://abetterrouteplanner.com)) for route and charging planning of electric vehicles. ABRP offers the possibility to create a personal account, specify your own vehicles, and also define a direct connection to the vehicle's account. However, this would again conflict with our initial goal: we would have to hand over the data of our Tesla account to a foreign provider. Through the collaboration of ABRP and TeslaLogger, there is a data-friendly way that is made available through this function.
