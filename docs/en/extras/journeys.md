# Journeys

You can use journeys to combine a long trip or period of time and summarize all numbers. This is very useful for tracking the total charging time of a long trip or comparing the consumption of summer or winter tires.

![IMAGE](/img/extras-journeys-01.png)

By clicking on the journey name, you are redirected to Grafana, where you can see, for example, the map of the journey:

![IMAGE](/img/extras-journeys-02.png)
