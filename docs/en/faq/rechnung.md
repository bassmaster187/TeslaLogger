# Download Invoices / Cancel Subscription / Change Payment Method
The payment service provider Stripe does not send invoices by itself to minimize email spam. Business customers need invoices for accounting, however.
To be able to download invoices, add a different payment method, or cancel the subscription, you must log in to Stripe with the stored email: https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/8wMaGogxma56fGUdQQ

Within seconds, you will receive a link where you can manage your subscription.
